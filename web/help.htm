Purpose:

Provide a AI control of one or more ships for the X-Wing Miniture Game

Setup:

Setup game per standard rules, choosing any squadrons and or missions.

Usage:

For each AI controlled ship:
  1. Select the faction of the the AI controlled ship
  2. Select the ship type for the AI controlled ship
  3. Pick a target (human controlled) ship for the AI ship to attach
  4. Select the location on the targeting ring that matches the direction of target (human) ship
  5. Move the AI Ship using the displayed maneuver
    • Choose the "Closing" maneuver if the Target ship is in range and facing towards the the AI ship
    • Choose the "Retreating" maneuver if the Target ship is in range and facing away from the AI ship
    • Choose the "Out-of-Range" maneuver if the Target ship is further than range 3 from the AI ship
    • Choose the "Stressed" maneuver if the AI ship has a
  6. Choose Action
  7. Complete round per standard rules

Picking Target:

There are multiple options for picking targets
  1. Always pick the nearest possible target
  2. Randomize target by rolling a 6-sided die:
    • 1-2: take on the closest one
    • 3-4: tako on the most damaged one
    • 5-6: take on the highest skilled pilot.

Choosing action:

Choose first action that matches criteria, based on list on the main page for the selected ship
NOTE: Target Lock is a free action, choose this plus first other action that is available in the list.

NOTES:

  • Always choose the human control ship(s) maneuvers, before choosing AI maneuvers
  • AI ships never get stress token, but do not perform any actions after a RED maneuver.
  • For secondary weapons or optional pilot skills, always use them when applicable
